Daily Coordinator Checklist

Use this list as a template for tasks to complete each day. Adjust or automate items as needed once the full workflow is integrated.

Morning

  • Review new mentor or student messages in the Communications hub.
  • Check pending roster updates from mentors.
  • Confirm upcoming deadlines in the milestone tracker.

Midday

  • Publish any resources or announcements scheduled for today.
  • Touch base with mentors assigned to students flagged for support.
  • Update coordinator notes with key decisions or blockers.

End of Day

  • Log outreach and follow-up items for tomorrow.
  • Send daily digest to mentors if needed.
  • Verify attendance or survey participation metrics.
